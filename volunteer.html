How Volunteering Contribute The Park?

Improve Cleanliness

Volunteers organize clean-up events, remove litter and repaint facilities, creating a safer and more welcoming park environment.

Repair & Maintain Equipment

Community members help report damages and assist in maintenance, keeping playground and exercise equipment safe and usable.

Plant Trees & Flowers

Tree-planting and gardening projects enhance beauty, improve air quality, and provide shade for visitors.

Increase Visitor Numbers

A clean and attractive park encourages more families, students, and community members to visit regularly.

Who Can Join the Volunteer?

STUDENTS & YOUTH

Every student has a responsibility to care for the environment. Young people can join to build leadership skills and help improve the community.

FAMILIES & ADULTS

Everyone has a responsibility to protect public spaces. Families and adults can volunteer together to create a cleaner, safer park.

COMMUNITY MEMBERS

Every community member shares the responsibility of maintaining local parks. By volunteering, we work together to keep our environment green and welcoming.
