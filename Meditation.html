Meditation Indoor and Outdoor

Comparing indoor and outdoor meditation

Meditation Indoor

01
Limited Space Indoors

Indoor meditation rooms are often small and restrict group participation.

02
Lack of Natural Environment

Indoor spaces limit exposure to fresh air, greenery, and natural sounds.

03
Low Community Engagement

Indoor meditation is often individual and less accessible to the public.

01
